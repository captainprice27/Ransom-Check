How It Works

Our advanced detection system uses deep learning algorithms to analyze APK files and identify potential ransomware threats with high accuracy. The model converts APKs to RGB images encoding their opcode sequences, textures, permissions and services. A CNN then processes these features to classify and flag malicious APKs, providing reliable detection results.
